Growing up Different: A Biography

Childhood Years ||         Teenage Years ||         College Years ||        

































Childhood Years

I grew up not having the same childhood experience as most Filipinos have. 1 I was a stay at home child basically watching english cartoons all day.2 When most Filipinos are playing outside, I just watch a lot of cartoons. 3 Back then my family wanted me to play an instrument so I started playing violin at the age of 7 in a local orchestra called “musikito”. 4 During this time I went to different parts of the Philippines performing as a 2nd violin player. 5 I fondly remember performing at popular places like CCP. 6And performing events including popular actors and actresses back then.7 One of the most popular actors I performed with is Regine Velasquez.8 It was during one of her concerts and we were asked to perform one of her background music in one of her songs.9 I was in musikito for 4-5 years before I got kicked because I could not perform at an event because of a family field trip.10 I also studied at a private school called Arelliano University but moved to Malabon Elementary School at grade 3 due to financial difficulties.11 I usually only go outside when I need to go to school or group practice sessions at my local orchestra. 12 Because of bad experiences of talking to strangers and bullying I got scared talking to strangers.13 Because of this I started going out less and just watched a lot of youtube and english cartoons.14 In the present time a lot of people I know are always shocked that I don't know anything about what is considered childhood for Filipinos.15 One of the biggest shocks for them is that I don't know anything about Voltes 5.16 They consider it as a staple childhood but for me I grew up to more of a western style childhood.17 That is why when my friends talk about their childhood experiences I am always confused or can’t relate.18 It takes a while to learn that you grew up very different from the norm.19 And to this day i still struggle that i am vastly different when talking to people. 20

Teenage Years

Teenage year was an afterthought for me.1 Before high school I joined a community marching band where I played trumpet while writing this. 2In a few weeks it will be my 10 year anniversary with this band. 3 The atmosphere in the community band I joined is a lot different compared to when I was in musikito. 4 Of course I was playing a new instrument but with my musical background I never had to relearn the basics and went straight to learning how to play the trumpet. 5 A lot of people in the community band thought my ear was somewhat special. 6 Because my ear can tell what is wrong really fast and can pick up things really quickly. 7 There was also an age gap here. 8 Back when I was in musikito most of the people i played with were within the same age group. 9 But at my community band I was playing with people who also have their dads playing for 20+ years in this community band. 10 I also played in different areas but it focuses more on festivals and marching which was really fun and tiring.11 In high school I went to a school called St.James Academy on a nightschool program which is a scholarship program that is sponsored by the DepEd. 12 During this time I never really made friends at my school and in my community band back then it was a lot more serious so I did not really socialize much. 13 St.James is a catholic school which unironically made me confirm to myself that I’m more of an atheist. 14 The upside of this school is that the tuition is free but the downside is that I have 5 years of high school instead of 4. 15 What's frustrating is that after I graduated they suddenly made it 4 years ago so I basically wasted a year. 16 During this time a year before covid, I started getting burnt out on playing trumpet. 17 So I made a decision to leave the community band. 18 I thought back then I was going to leave indefinitely but in 2023 some people asked me if i wanted to practice again which i said sure because it has been a while since i played. 19 Senior High school was not really different. 20 I still did not socialize much and mostly focused on studying which really made me mentally messed up. 21 Because I always ignored socializing and because of that I was socially awkward and still scared of talking to people. 22

College Years

Before College I made a rule for myself to be out of my comfort zone and socialize more. 1 Which made College probably the most eventful I have ever been. 2 I was not planning on going to U.E i was thinking of probably going to NU or AdU. 3 But because of my community band, my friends suggested that I try and audition at a U.E band. 4 Because if I pass the audition I can get a scholarship from the school. 5 I had low expectations because I was rusty and had not played in 4-5 years. 6 I also could not start practicing because I was working on my final research project. 7 So when the audition ended they announced I was actually part of the U.E band. 8 I was surprised and also got a lot of imposter syndrome which took a while to adjust to. 9 During 1st year of college I started talking to people like, like a lot, which made me more social but I realized I am not good at it. 10 I was getting more tired socializing than doing some of my assignments. 11 College is also a wake up call for me as a student. 12 My mindset has always been that school is all about studying. 13 But it is also about making connections and learning to be more social. 14 It also showcased how draining college is with all the work you have to do to survive. 15 I understand why people say that college is where you truly test your will and also if you truly enjoy the course you took. 16 It really tackles you to be more out of your comfort zone which is for me something I like and dislike. 17 College is also the way to make connections and truly be out there. 18 2nd year for me was a lot more difficult than first year because you are losing more free time because work does not help. 19 I have community band practice, U.E band practice and school work balancing all of the same time. 20 But it's fun and I am learning to adjust to it. 21
